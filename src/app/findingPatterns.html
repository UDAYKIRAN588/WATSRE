Overview of the Process

Click on the each step button in the order of the numbers and perform accordingly

Step 4 : The reduced instructions are displayed here in the Output instruction area

Step 5 : In this results area, the summary of instructions and sequences, the sequences list are shown

Step 6 : Click on the 'Open Full view' button in oder to see the results in larger screen

Step 7 : click on the 'Open sequences chart to view the bar chart of the sequences identified
